**Version:** 1.0

# Purpose

This document describes the complete weekly workflow of a ride-hailing driver.

The goal is to ensure that every screen, calculation, and feature in Biyahe follows the driver's real operational routine.

---

# Weekly Driver Journey

The driver does not think in terms of monthly accounting.

The driver's business revolves around one cycle:

Monday ➜ Sunday

Every week starts with working capital and ends with weekly settlement.

---

# MONDAY - Start of Business Week

## Goal

Prepare the business for a new week.

## Activities

• Receive working capital
• Fuel the vehicle
• Prepare change money
• Buy meals if necessary
• Start driving

₱1,000

# User Experience Goals

The driver should always know:

✓ Am I making money?

✓ Am I behind on rental?

✓ How much can I safely take home?

✓ Where did my money go?

✓ Do my cash balances match my records?

without opening Excel.

---

# Success Criteria

A successful user journey means:

• Daily recording takes less than one minute.
• Weekly closing takes less than five minutes.
• No manual calculator.
• No Excel.
• No guessing.
• No missing money.
