# BIYAHE - Product Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft

---

# Vision

Build the most trusted financial operating system for Filipino ride-hailing drivers.

---

# Product Strategy

Biyahe will be developed in phases.

Each phase delivers value to drivers while preparing the platform for future growth.

Our philosophy is:

**Release early. Improve continuously.**

# Long-Term Vision

Biyahe becomes the standard business management platform for ride-hailing drivers in the Philippines.

Future expansion may include:

- Grab
- JoyRide
- inDrive
- Taxi
- Delivery Drivers
- Logistics Drivers

---

# Success Metrics

Version 1.0

- Daily recording under 1 minute
- Weekly closing under 5 minutes
- Eliminate manual Excel tracking
- Accurate weekly rental computation
- Positive feedback from pilot users

---

# Guiding Principles

Every feature must:

- Solve a real driver problem.
- Be simple to use.
- Reduce manual work.
- Improve financial visibility.
- Support configurable operator policies.

---

# Project Motto

**Built by drivers. Built with drivers. Built for drivers.**
