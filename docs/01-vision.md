# BIYAHE - Product Vision

# Vision Statement

To become the most trusted financial operating system for Filipino ride-hailing drivers by helping them understand, manage, and grow their driving business through simple, accurate, and intelligent financial management.

---

# Mission

Biyahe exists to help every driver finish the week with confidence.

Instead of wondering if they have enough money for rental, fuel, or take-home income, drivers will have a complete financial picture of their business through one simple mobile application.

Built from real driving experience, Biyahe transforms daily earnings into meaningful financial insights that help drivers make better decisions every week.

---

# The Problem

Many ride-hailing drivers know how much they earned today, but very few know:

- How much they actually earned for the week
- Whether they are behind on rental
- Whether they have already recovered their working capital
- Their true business profit after expenses
- How much money they can safely take home
- Whether their Cash on Hand, Grab Wallet, and GCash balances are correct
- How operator-approved deductions affect their rental

Most drivers still rely on manual computations, Excel spreadsheets, calculators, or family members to reconcile their weekly finances.

This process is time-consuming, error-prone, and stressful.

---

# Our Solution

Biyahe is a mobile-first financial management platform built specifically for Filipino ride-hailing drivers.

The application simplifies daily recording and automatically manages:

- Weekly earnings
- Working capital
- Rental obligations
- Operating expenses
- Personal expenses
- Cash reconciliation
- Weekly financial closing
- Operator-specific rental rules

Instead of spending time computing, drivers spend less than one minute recording their day while Biyahe performs the calculations automatically.

---

# Our Philosophy

We are not building another expense tracker.

We are building a business management platform designed around the real workflow of ride-hailing drivers.

Every feature must answer one simple question:

> **"Will this help a driver finish the week with confidence?"**

If the answer is no, the feature does not belong in the product.

---

# Core Principles

## Driver First

Every feature must solve a real problem experienced by drivers.

---

## Simplicity

Daily recording should take less than one minute.

---

## Accuracy

Financial calculations must accurately reflect real business operations.

---

## Flexibility

Different operators have different policies.

Biyahe must support configurable business rules instead of hardcoded assumptions.

Examples include:

- Sunday Free Rental
- Legal Holiday Free Rental
- Weekly Settlement
- Operator-approved rental deductions

---

## Transparency

Drivers should always know:

- How much they earned
- How much they owe
- How much they can safely take home

without manual calculations.

---

# Success Metrics

A successful Biyahe user should be able to:

- Complete daily recording in under one minute.
- Know their weekly rental status instantly.
- Eliminate manual Excel reconciliation.
- Generate a weekly financial summary with one tap.
- Understand their real business profit before taking money home.
