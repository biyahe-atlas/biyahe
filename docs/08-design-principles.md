# BIYAHE - Design Principles

Version: 1.0

---

## Philosophy

A driver should understand their financial status within three seconds of opening the app.

---

## Principle 1

One-Hand Operation

The application should be usable with one hand while sitting inside the vehicle.

---

## Principle 2

Large Numbers

Drivers care about numbers.

Important financial values must be large and easy to read.

---

## Principle 3

Weekly First

Everything revolves around the current week.

---

## Principle 4

Less Typing

Typing should be minimized.

Use selections, buttons, and presets whenever possible.

---

## Principle 5

One Minute Closing

Daily recording should never exceed one minute.

---

## Principle 6

Every Peso Has a Story

Every peso should always be traceable.

---

## Principle 7

No Accounting Terms

Use language drivers already understand.

Instead of:

Business Profit

Consider:

My Earnings

or

Take Home

when appropriate.

---

## Principle 8

Show Progress

Drivers are motivated by progress.

Show rental completion visually.

---

## Principle 9

Simple Colors

Green

Healthy

Orange

Needs Attention

Red

Action Needed

---

## Principle 10

Confidence

The application should reduce stress instead of creating it.
